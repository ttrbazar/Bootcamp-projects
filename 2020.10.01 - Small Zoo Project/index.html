Zoo Application

Long-Tailed Chinchillas Meal: 1 bag animal pellets, 1 bag dried fruit, 1/2 bag cashews, 5 carrots, 3 stalks kale Times: 8:00 am Directions: disperse contents throughout Chinchilla habitat
Western Lowland Gorilla Meal: 20 lbs. kale, 10 lbs. celery, 10 lbs. green beans, 5 lbs. carrots, 1 bag sweet potatoes. Times: 6:30 am, 12:00 pm, 7:00 pm Directions: feed Gorillas in the morning as group, spread forage items during noon meal, and divide quantities for individual feeding in evening
